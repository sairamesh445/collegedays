Embarking on an industrial tour in Vizag with friends promises an adventure-packed journey filled with exploration and camaraderie. Our itinerary includes a visit to the
renowned Shimalachalam Steel Plant, where we'll gain insights into the manufacturing process and witness cutting-edge technology in action. Afterwards, we'll ascend to the picturesque Kailasagiri hill station, offering breathtaking panoramic views of the city and coastline. Our accommodation
will be at RK Beach Rooms, providing a comfortable stay amidst the tranquil seaside ambiance. The next leg of our journey takes us to the enchanting Araku Valley, where we'll explore the mystical Borra Caves, marveling at their stunning limestone formations. A highlight awaits at the majestic

Farewell Vibes






Organizing a memorable farewell for BVC College's B.Tech seniors involves careful planning and thoughtful touches.
We'll select a theme that encapsulates their college journey, transforming the venue with nostalgic decor. Entertainment options like student performances and talent showcases will keep spirits high.
Speeches and tributes from juniors and faculty will express gratitude, while awards and recognitions will highlight seniors' achievements.
Photo displays and interactive activities will foster reminiscence and connection. A delicious dinner and refreshments will facilitate mingling,
and personalized gifts will serve as tokens of appreciation. Finally, an open mic or message wall will allow both juniors and seniors
to share their sentiments and memories, ensuring a heartfelt farewell filled with love and appreciation.




Vadapalli Vibes


Exploring Vadapalli Temple with friends promises an enriching and spiritually uplifting experience. As we enter the temple

The temple's architecture, adorned with intricate carvings and colorful decorations, captivates our senses and fills us with awe. Together, we embark on a journey of devotion, offering our prayers and seeking blessings from the divine. The tranquil surroundings and
the sanctity of the temple create a sense of peace and
tranquility within us, allowing us to connect with our inner selves and find solace amidst the chaos of life.
